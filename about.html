Our Story

The Legacy of Kashmiri Craftsmanship

Rooted in the Valley

Chinar Craft House was born out of a deep love for the rich cultural heritage of Kashmir. Named after the majestic Chinar tree, whose leaves turn a brilliant gold and maroon in autumn, our brand reflects the beauty and resilience of the valley.

For centuries, Kashmiri artisans have woven magic with their hands. From the delicate embroidery of Pashmina shawls to the intricate patterns of traditional knitwear, every piece is a labor of love that takes days, if not months, to complete.

We work directly with these master artisans, ensuring fair trade practices while bringing you authentic, high-quality, and ethically sourced handmade products.

Our Promise

We promise to deliver 100% authentic Kashmiri handmade products, preserving the art of slow fashion and supporting the local artisan communities of the valley.
